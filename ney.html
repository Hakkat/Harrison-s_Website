Who is Neymar?

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior is a professional football player from São Paulo, Brazil. He currently plays for French club Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) and the Brazil National Team.

Widely considered one of the greatest players of his generation, Neymar boasts a stellar career at both the club and national level. He has played for some of the most decorated clubs in the world – most notably FC Barcelona and Paris Saint-Germain – and is best known for his technical ability, flair, and creativity.
